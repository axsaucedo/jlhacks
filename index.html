About JLHacks


Innovating the retail industry

JLHacks is John Lewis' flagship hackathon aimed to innovate the retail industry. A high impact hackathon, where 50 of the best developers, designers and industry experts from all around Europe will conceive, build and launch solutions that will revolutionize retail. During this JLHacks, solutions will be targeted to innovate payments in the retail industry.


At the John Lewis Oxford Circus store

Located in the heart of London, the JLHacks hackathon will take place at the main John Lewis store in Oxford Circus. This will be the perfect venue, offering a unique, immersive experience, allowing participants to be immerse in the same industry that they will be innovating throughout the weekend. This will be the perfect environment to create the next revolutionary Startups.


Cutting edge gadgets, APIs, Datasets

We will be providing a set of gadgets, access to APIs and Datasets relevant to the retail industry that will help participants in their Startup journeys. Throughout the whole weekend, we will provide access to 3D printers, Google Glass, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Myo Devices, and a few other gadgets. We'll have access to several APIs, and a few Datasets will be made available for this event.


Unbelievable speakers, judges and mentors

Kick-starting the hackathon with talks from senior excecutives from leading retail companies and corporations. Mentors consist of technology and industry professionals. The judging panel formed by VCs, investors and industry experts. JLHacks aims to provide the right environment to ensure all attendees have access to expert advice and judgement throughout the whole event, in order for them to truly build software that will revolutionize retail as we know it.


Let's make this happen

Organized by Hack Partners - the same organization that ran the StartupBus UK 2015, the HackTrain 2015, StartupHacks, HackaGlobal, between tenths of other successful hackathons - this hackathon will certainly be full of amazing surprises that will amaze all the participants. JLHacks is the largest impact UK hackathon targeted for Retail Innovation! An unforgettable experience where 50 of the best developers from all around Europe will change the world! Located in the heart of London, this hackathon will allow these young bright minds to hack inside the John Lewis stores to create and build products that will revolutionize the retail industry!
