About JLHacks

JLHacks is the largest impact UK hackathon targeted for Retail Innovation! An unforgettable experience where 50 of the best developers from all around Europe will change the world! Located in the heart of London, this hackathon will allow these young bright minds to hack inside the John Lewis stores to create and build products that will revolutionize the retail industry!


Startups, apps, prototypes will be created, and presented in front of CEOs, investors, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists - all with the objective of becoming the 2015 JLHacks champion! With attendees from UK, Russia, Romania, France, Poland, Czech Republic together with many other European countries, this hackathon will certainly promise a unique experience!


Crazy? Definitely! Impossible? Not even close! Many individuals rocked our past events, creating mind blowing and revolutionary concepts! Some of our past events include The HackTrain, Startup Bus UK 2014, Startup Weekend Moscow, Startup Hacks.


Thanks to our strong partnership with one of the largest retailers in the UK, John Lewis, attendees will be able to hack away inside their stores in central London!
